What I've built

AI managed meetings

For a medium sized business, I integrated a scattered Microsoft 365 setup with AI that runs their weekly team meeting end to end.

The hard part is pulling the right context, connecting the right tools, and producing output the team trusts every time.

Now they own a system that builds their agenda, captures the full discussion, and files new tasks in MS Planner.

Academic AI research

As lead developer I architected the Python backend behind an academic writing AI. My team and I took it from prototype to production, past 50,000 monthly active users.

The heart of it was the research engine. A web of APIs was wired together and ran every result through layers of filtering and AI curation, so each paper drew on only the strongest, most relevant sources.

A game world run by AI

A multiplayer RPG where a team of AI agents narrates and builds the story in real time, driving the plot, summarizing to hold the lore, and spawning assets to paint the world as it unfolds, each anchored to the same shared game state. The story holds together across hours of open-ended play.

FastAPI orchestrates the agents, a Lua frontend renders the world, an n8n pipeline talks to illustrators on the fly.
